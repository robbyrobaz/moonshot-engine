## Recommendation

Recommendation: remove social signals from Moonshot model search space for now.

Reason:
- No historical positions were generated by social-enabled models.
- Social-enabled models underperform non-social models on stored backtest metrics.
- The only active social-enabled models assign zero importance to all social features.
- Disabling social features produced identical FT replay results for the active social cohort.

Actionable next steps:
- Default challenger generation to `no_social` and stop sampling `all` until a redesigned social feature set is available.
- Keep the new `MOONSHOT_DISABLE_SOCIAL_FEATURES` switch for future DRY_RUN audits.
- If social is revisited, redesign the inputs first. Current signals are likely too sparse/global and not coin-specific enough to help.
